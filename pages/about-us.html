About Wagle Universal Solutions

A full-stack software team building real products for real businesses.

Our Story

Founded by an engineer
who builds products.

Wagle Universal Solutions (WUSPL) was founded in 2024 by Anish Wagle — a full-stack developer with experience across Angular, Next.js, .NET, and Node.js. What started as a one-person consultancy has grown into a team-ready operation serving clients internationally.

We've built and shipped production applications for clients in the United Kingdom and Australia, and developed our own SaaS product, AstroEngine.ai — an AI-powered Vedic astrology platform. This hands-on product-building experience informs every project we take on.
