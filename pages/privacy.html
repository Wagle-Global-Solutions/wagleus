Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Wagle Universal Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website (www.wagleus.com) and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and message content when you use our contact form.
  • Usage Data: We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our website (page views, device type, referrer).

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and project requests
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send project-related communications (only if you've contacted us first)

Developer Tools

Our free developer tools (JSON formatter, password generator, etc.) process all data entirely in your browser. No data from these tools is sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

Third-Party Services

  • Web3Forms: Our contact form uses Web3Forms to deliver submissions. Their privacy policy applies to form submissions.
  • GitHub Pages: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's privacy policy applies to hosting.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Our website does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking tools.

Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all communications. We do not store personal data in databases — contact form submissions are delivered directly to our email.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us at info@wagleus.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@wagleus.com.
